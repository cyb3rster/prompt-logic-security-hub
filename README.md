### 🌐 MISSION MANIFESTO
Maintained by **[Umer Imran](https://github.com/cyb3rster)** (Founder of **Prompt Logic Research**), this vault is a premier open-source intelligence (OSINT) resource dedicated to the architecture and security of modern AI systems. 

Our objective is to provide a transparent look into the system-level instructions that govern today's most powerful LLMs. This repository is curated for **AI Security Researchers**, **Red Teamers**, and **Prompt Architects** who seek to understand the boundaries of model behavior.

---

### 🛡️ SECURITY ADVISORY: AI SYSTEM INTEGRITY
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Advisory:** In the era of autonomous agents, the confidentiality of system prompts is a critical security layer. Instruction leakage often serves as the initial vector for complex prompt injection attacks, unauthorized tool orchestration, and data exfiltration.
>
> This repository serves as a research baseline. Organizations are advised to adopt **Zero-Trust AI Architectures**, implement robust output sanitization, and conduct regular adversarial testing to mitigate the risks associated with prompt-level vulnerabilities.
